Carmen Paun
Nice to meet you! I am a front-end developer.
Hello! My name is Carmen Paun, and I am 25 years old. I was born in Bucharest, Romania and recently moved to London, UK. I would describe myself as being an optimist person, driven, funny (at least I hope so), very organized and an avid reader with a great imagination.
I first discovered my passion for front-end development two years ago, when I was working as a product manager at a consulting company.
It was mostly curiosity at first, but then I got a real taste for it and decided to dedicate myself full-time to this passion. I enrolled into as many tutorials as I could. I have studied HTML, CSS and SASS, JavaScript and jQuery. I even received a scholarship given by Google in collaboration with Udacity, which helped me achieve even more knowledge about front-end development and also helped me understand more ReactJs.
My main goal is to constantly better myself and create beautiful, easy-to-use websites.
